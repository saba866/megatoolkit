FAQ

What makes a strong password?

A strong password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

Are my passwords stored?

No, passwords are generated locally in your browser and are never stored or transmitted.

How often should I change passwords?

It's recommended to change passwords every 3-6 months or immediately if you suspect a security breach.
