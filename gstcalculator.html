Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST?
GST is a value-added tax levied on most goods and services sold for domestic consumption in India. It is a comprehensive indirect tax that has replaced many indirect taxes.
How do I use this calculator?
Enter the base amount of your product or service. Select the applicable GST rate from the dropdown. Choose whether you want to calculate GST to be added (Exclusive) or removed (Inclusive) from the base amount. Click 'Calculate GST' to see the results.
What are CGST, SGST, and IGST?

CGST (Central Goods and Services Tax): Tax levied on intra-state supplies of goods and services by the Central Government.

SGST (State Goods and Services Tax): Tax levied on intra-state supplies of goods and services by the State Government.

IGST (Integrated Goods and Services Tax): Tax levied on inter-state supplies of goods and services by the Central Government. IGST is the sum of CGST and SGST.

This calculator shows CGST and SGST for simplicity, assuming an intra-state transaction where GST is split equally between central and state governments.

Can I calculate GST for different rates?
Yes, the calculator provides common GST rates (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) for you to select from.
What is the difference between Exclusive and Inclusive GST?

Exclusive GST: The GST amount is added to the base amount to get the total amount. This is common for B2B transactions.

Inclusive GST: The base amount already includes the GST. The calculator will back-calculate the GST amount and the original amount before tax. This is common for B2C transactions where the displayed price is the final price.
