Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP investment? +

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It helps in rupee cost averaging and disciplined investing.

How does this calculator work? +

The calculator uses compound interest formula to project your investment growth based on monthly contributions, expected return rate, and investment duration.

Are the results guaranteed? +

No, these are projected values based on your inputs. Actual returns may vary depending on market performance.
