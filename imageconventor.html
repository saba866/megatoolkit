Frequently Asked Questions

What image file types can I upload?

Currently, you can upload JPG and PNG image files.

What formats can I convert to?

You can convert your uploaded JPG or PNG images to JPG, PNG, PDF, and DOCX formats.

How does the conversion work?

The image is loaded into your browser, and the conversion is performed using JavaScript and browser APIs (like Canvas, jsPDF, and docx). The converted file is then generated for download directly from your browser.

Is my data safe?

Yes, your files are processed entirely within your browser. They are not uploaded to any server, ensuring your privacy and data security.
