About This Tool

This Loan EMI Calculator helps you determine the monthly installment you need to pay towards your loan. By inputting the principal loan amount, the annual interest rate, and the loan tenure in months, the calculator provides you with the exact EMI amount, the total interest payable, and the total amount payable. It also generates a detailed amortization schedule showing the breakdown of principal and interest for each month.

This tool is useful for financial planning, comparing different loan offers, and understanding how your loan is repaid over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMI?
EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month. EMIs are used to pay off both interest and principal every month, so that over a specified number of years, the loan is fully paid off.
How is EMI calculated?
The formula for calculating EMI is: P * r * (1 + r)^n / ((1 + r)^n – 1), where P is the principal loan amount, r is the monthly interest rate (annual rate divided by 12 and then by 100), and n is the loan tenure in months.
What is an amortization schedule?
An amortization schedule is a table detailing each periodic payment on an amortizing loan (typically a mortgage or car loan), as generated by an amortization calculator. It shows the amount of principal and interest included in each payment and the remaining loan balance after each payment.
Can I use this calculator for different loan types?
Yes, this calculator can be used for any type of amortizing loan, including home loans, car loans, personal loans, and education loans, as long as you have the principal amount, annual interest rate, and loan tenure.
Is my data stored?
No, your input data is processed locally in your browser and is not stored or transmitted anywhere.
