Frequently Asked Questions

What image file types are supported?

We support common image formats like JPG, PNG, and GIF.

How do I resize an image?

Upload your image, enter the desired width and height in the input fields, and click the "Resize Image" button. A preview of the resized image will appear below.

Will resizing affect image quality?

Resizing can sometimes affect quality, especially when significantly increasing the size (upscaling). Our tool aims to maintain quality, but for best results, avoid extreme upscaling.

Is my data safe?

Yes, your files are processed directly in your browser. They are not uploaded to our servers, ensuring your privacy and data security.
